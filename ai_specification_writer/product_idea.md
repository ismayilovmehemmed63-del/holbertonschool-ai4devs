# Product Idea

## Vision
Enable students and professionals to connect and share rides efficiently,
reducing transportation costs, traffic congestion, and carbon emissions
through an intelligent AI-powered carpooling platform.

## Users
- **Drivers**: Want to fill empty seats, share fuel costs, and reduce
  the financial burden of daily commuting.
- **Passengers**: Want affordable, reliable, and safe rides to work,
  university, or other destinations.
- **Administrators**: Want to monitor platform activity, manage users,
  and ensure safety and compliance.

## Key Features
- Real-time ride matching based on location and schedule
- In-app payment integration with split cost calculation
- User ratings and reviews for drivers and passengers
- Eco-impact tracking showing CO2 emissions saved
- AI-powered route optimization for efficient carpooling
- In-app chat and notifications for ride coordination
- Safety features including identity verification and emergency contacts
