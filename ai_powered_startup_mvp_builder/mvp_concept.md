# MVP Concept - AI Study Planner

## Problem Statement
Students often struggle to manage their study schedules effectively, leading to burnout or poor academic performance. Traditional calendars require manual entry and don't adapt to changing workloads.

## Target Users
- University students
- Lifelong learners
- High school students preparing for exams

## Core Features
- Automatic schedule generation based on exam dates.
- AI-driven priority setting for different subjects.
- Daily notification system for upcoming tasks.
- Progress tracking dashboard.

## Constraints
- No third-party calendar integration (e.g., Google Calendar) in MVP.
- Maximum of 5 active courses per user.
- Local storage only (no cloud database for now).
